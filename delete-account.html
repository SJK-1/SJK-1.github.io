Delete Account & Data

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data as follows:

  1. In-app (preferred): Profile → Settings → Delete account. This signs you out and schedules deletion of your authentication profile and stored counters.
  2. By email: Send a request to salmajaoua@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We’ll confirm and complete deletion.

Deletion applies to your authentication profile and stored counters/images. Some logs may be retained for a limited time for security and legal compliance.

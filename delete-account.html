Delete Account & Data

How to permanently remove your BactCounter account and associated data.

Delete directly in the app (preferred)

  1. Open BactCounter → Profile → Settings → Delete account.
  2. Confirm. You’ll be signed out; deletion of your authentication profile and stored counters/images is scheduled.

If you don’t see this option yet, use the email method below.

Request deletion by email

  1. Send an email to salmajaoua@gmail.com from the address associated with your account.
  2. Subject: BactCounter Account Deletion
  3. Include: your full name (optional) and confirmation you want your account deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Your authentication profile (Google Sign-In identifiers).
  • Stored counters, sessions, and uploaded images associated with your account.

Some minimal security logs may be retained briefly as required by law and for abuse prevention.

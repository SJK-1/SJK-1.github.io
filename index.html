BactCounter

Purpose: BactCounter is a mobile application that helps you count bacterial colonies from Petri dish images. Capture or upload photos, let the app detect and count colonies, review and edit results, and export summaries. Your counters and sessions can be synced securely across devices.

What the App Does

  • Capture or upload plate images for automatic colony detection & counting.
  • Review counts, adjust thresholds, and save named sessions.
  • Sync your results to restore them across devices.
  • Export counts and images for lab notes, reports, and teaching.

Google data we request & why

BactCounter uses Google Sign-In (Firebase Authentication) solely to authenticate you and sync your saved counters.

ScopeWhy it’s needed
openidIdentify your account securely and maintain a session.
emailAssociate your saved counters with your account and send account-related messages if needed.
profileDisplay your display name/avatar in the app UI (if available).

No other Google scopes (e.g., Gmail, Drive, Calendar) are requested.

We do not sell personal data.

Data handling summary

  • Collection: basic profile, email, and authentication identifiers from Google; counters and images you upload.
  • Use: authenticate, sync, and restore your counters; provide app functionality.
  • Sharing: no selling of data. Third-party processors (Google/Firebase) provide authentication and storage.
  • Retention: data is kept while your account remains active. You can delete your data at any time.
  • Deletion: see Delete Account for automated and email-based deletion options.

BactCounter

Purpose: BactCounter is a camera-based colony counter. Capture or upload plate images, automatically detect and count colonies, and manage multiple counters for lab experiments or inventories. Results are saved and synced across your devices.

What BactCounter Does

  • Capture or upload plate images to detect and count colonies.
  • Save, name, and manage multiple counters and sessions.
  • Review, export, and continue analyses across devices.

How Google Sign-In Is Used

BactCounter uses Google Identity / Firebase Auth to authenticate you and sync your data.

  • Data requested: basic profile (name), email address, and Google account ID (openid, email, profile scopes).
  • How we use it: create your account, keep you signed in, save/restore your counters across devices.
  • What we don’t do: we do not sell personal data.

See our Privacy Policy for details and data-deletion options.

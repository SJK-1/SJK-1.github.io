Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 Oct 2025

Who we are

BactCounter is a mobile application for counting bacterial colonies from Petri dish images. Contact: salmajaoua@gmail.com.

Data we collect

  • Google Sign-In data: basic profile, email, and identifiers (scopes: openid, email, profile) to authenticate you and maintain your account.
  • User content: images you upload, counters, and session metadata you save in the app.
  • Diagnostic data: basic, aggregate logs and crash reports to improve stability (where enabled by your device settings).

How we use data

  • Authenticate you and keep your session active.
  • Sync and restore your counters across devices.
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app (including troubleshooting).

Sharing and processors

We do not sell personal data. We use trusted processors to provide services:

  • Google/Firebase — authentication and cloud storage. See Google’s policies and terms.

Retention

We retain your account data and saved counters while your account remains active. If you delete your account or request deletion, we delete associated personal data, subject to lawful retention requirements.

Your choices

  • Export or delete content from within the app where available.
  • Request deletion or account closure via the Delete Account page or by emailing salmajaoua@gmail.com.

Security

Data is transmitted over HTTPS. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.

Google API Services User Data Policy

BactCounter’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
